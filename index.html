Background

Summary: Latitude vs. X

Through the use of the OpenWeatherAPI and the CitiPy Python libray, weather data for over 544 random cities was retrieved and a dataset was assembled.

After assembly of the dataset, Python's Matplotlib library was used in order to explore the relationship between different facets of a city's weather data and its latitude. Some of the factors that were evaluated were: temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind-speed. This site provides the source data along with visualizations for the aforementioned relationships created as part of the analysis along with explanations and descriptons of any observed correlations and trends.
