Summary: Latitude vs. Windspeed

There seems to be little to no correlation between latitude and windspeed. Cities at different latitudes seem to have all different average wind speeds; however, it does seem that a majority of cities all across the globes seem to have a windspeed less than 10 mph which might be just due to human preference. It is most likely true that our human predecessors decided to settle where there was lower windspeeds which resulting in the future cities that would build atop the old settlements to also have low average windspeeds.
